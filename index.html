What TreeNet Is

A parental control app centered on digital wellbeing.

TreeNet helps parents manage their children's screen time and understand how devices are being used day to day. Parents can connect a child's device, review usage insights, and set appropriate limits that encourage healthier routines.

The app also includes optional motivation systems for children, including games and progression mechanics that reward reduced screen time and more balanced habits.

Why It Matters

TreeNet is built to teach balance, not just enforce limits.

The long-term goal is to help children understand that life should include more than screens and scrolling. TreeNet supports that goal by combining parental controls with positive habit-building.

For Apple's Family Controls review context, TreeNet clearly serves a parental oversight purpose: it connects parent and child devices, provides visibility into usage, and helps families manage screen time in a healthier and more intentional way.
